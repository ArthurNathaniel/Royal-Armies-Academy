School Rules and Regulations

All students are required to adhere to the following rules and regulations to maintain a safe, respectful, and productive learning environment. Failure to follow these guidelines may result in disciplinary action.

General Rules

  • All students must wear the prescribed school uniform at all times while on school premises.
  • Students should arrive at school on time and attend all classes punctually.
  • Absences must be justified by a written note from a parent or guardian, and prolonged absences require permission from the school administration.
  • Students must respect school property, including classrooms, furniture, equipment, and all learning resources.

Academic Rules

  • Students are expected to complete all assignments, projects, and homework on time.
  • Academic dishonesty, including cheating on exams and plagiarism, is strictly prohibited.
  • All students must attend mandatory classes, labs, and extracurricular activities unless excused by a teacher or administrator.
  • Students should strive to meet academic performance standards and seek help from teachers when needed.

Behavioral Expectations

  • Students must show respect to teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  • Bullying, harassment, and discrimination of any kind are not tolerated.
  • The use of offensive language, fighting, and disruptive behavior is prohibited on school grounds.
  • Cell phones and electronic devices should be turned off during classes and school events unless permitted by a teacher.
  • Smoking, alcohol, and drug use are strictly forbidden on school property.

Attendance and Punctuality

  • Students must be present for at least 90% of school days per term to qualify for end-of-term exams.
  • Repeated tardiness or absence without a valid reason may result in disciplinary action.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Violation of school rules may result in consequences such as verbal warnings, detention, or suspension, depending on the severity of the infraction.
  • Parents or guardians may be called in for a meeting in cases of serious or repeated offenses.
  • In cases of severe misconduct, expulsion may be considered.

Health and Safety Regulations

  • Students are required to report any safety hazards or injuries to a teacher or staff member immediately.
  • All students must participate in fire drills and other safety procedures as instructed.
  • Students should maintain personal hygiene and adhere to health protocols provided by the school.

Other Important Guidelines

  • Students are encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities and represent the school in a positive manner.
  • All students must follow school transportation rules while using school buses.
  • Students are responsible for all personal belongings, and the school will not be liable for lost or stolen items.

Note: These rules are subject to change. Students and parents will be informed of any updates to the rules and regulations. Compliance with these guidelines will ensure a safe and conducive learning environment for all.
