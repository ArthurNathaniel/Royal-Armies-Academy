Prospectus - General Items

Uniforms

  • School uniforms (number and specifics depend on school policy)
  • House attire or sportswear
  • White shirt/blouse and khaki shorts/skirts (for some schools)

Clothing and Bedding

  • Bed sheets (often specified size)
  • Pillow and pillowcases
  • Blanket
  • Mosquito net
  • Towels

Footwear

  • Black school shoes
  • White sneakers (for sports and Physical Education)
  • Sandals or slippers

Books and Stationery

  • Exercise books and notebooks
  • Pens, pencils, ruler, eraser, and sharpener
  • Graph books (especially for science students)
  • Mathematical set
  • Scientific calculator (for science and business students)
  • Textbooks (as specified by the school)

Personal Hygiene Products

  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Bathing soap and sponge
  • Toilet roll
  • Washing powder or soap
  • Bucket and soap dish
  • Deodorant

Household Essentials

  • Chop box (storage box for food items and belongings)
  • Trunk or suitcase
  • Plates, bowls, cutlery (spoons, forks, etc.)
  • Cup or mug
  • Flask (for hot water)

Food Supplies

  • Gari, sugar, milk powder, and cocoa powder
  • Cereal (e.g., cornflakes)
  • Snacks (e.g., biscuits, groundnuts)
  • Canned foods (optional, as some schools restrict these items)

Other Essentials

  • Broom (hard and soft types for cleaning)
  • Mop and bucket (if required)
  • Iron (check if permitted)
  • Padlocks and keys (for securing chop box and trunk)
  • School bag or backpack
  • Torch or flashlight (with extra batteries)

Medical Kit

  • Basic first aid kit (e.g., plasters, pain relievers, antiseptic)
  • Health insurance card

Miscellaneous

  • Pocket money (for personal expenses)
  • Handkerchiefs and face towels
  • Bible or Quran (depending on the schoolâ€™s religious affiliation)

Subject-Specific Requirements (If Applicable)

  • Science Students: Lab coat, graph book
  • Home Economics Students: Sewing kit, apron, fabric, cooking utensils
  • Visual Arts Students: Drawing materials (e.g., pencils, colors, brushes)

Always check with the specific school for their updated prospectus as items can vary based on school policies.
